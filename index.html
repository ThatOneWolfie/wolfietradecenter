About Wolfie Trade Center

Wolfie Trade Center is a trade center in FoxSurvival. It was originally made by WolfieW and still is. It started out as a small shop where you could buy things for iron and coal and gold and such. Once the global currency for FoxSurvival got updated, so did our currency. Now you can buy food and animals and so much more for cheap! No more having to go to other places and find out they are too expensive for you. The minimum thing you can buy is a carrot for 1 Gold Nugget. That is so cheap! You should really consider going there. Our Address is 464 Wolf Lane, FoxVillage, FS. Check it out!

Where we get our items.

At Wolfie's Trade Center, we have our items fresh. We don't wait for them to become stale. We freshly pick out our food and we breed our animals for freshness. We sell the freshest stuff on the market. You don't have to go to a expensive place to get a carrot or a cow. Just head on over to Wolfie's Trade Center. We sell the freshest stuff on the market!
